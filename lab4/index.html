Lab 4: Pseudocoding and Problem-Solving

Challenge

Write psuedocode for two tasks: an everyday task and a simple game. Then, input the latter into an HTML file.

Problems

The main problems were towards proper phrasing and making sure not to break the pseudocode down too much or make it too complex.

Reflection

I thought this assignment was pretty interesting and fun, and I've written pseudocode before in other classes, so I knew the gist of what we were doing. I feel that we put much more effort into this assignment than was necessary by using images and links to outside sources.
